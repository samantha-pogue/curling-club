Welcome to the Curling Club at the University of Minnesota


We are a student group that's trying to promote the great sport of curling on campus, and we compete against the curling clubs of other universities across the midwest.

If you haven't curled before, we hold regular practices where we can teach you how curling works and so you can practice your skills. Our season runs from late October to mid-March.
