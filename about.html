About Our Club


From its Scottish roots to becoming one of the fastest growing sports in North America, curling is a sport that can be played by 4 year olds and 94 year olds at the same time. With a great mix of strategy and physical control, curling is something you can enjoy for the rest of your life.

The Curling Club will provide the members of the University community regular access to ice time, equipment, and lessons. An interested team or multiple teams will regularly compete in bonspiels against other universities to acquire merit points for a national ranking. The Curling Club will host a bonspiel for other university teams to compete at in late November.
